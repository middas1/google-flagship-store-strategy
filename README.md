# Google Flagship Store — Market & Digital Strategy

## Overview

A strategic market analysis project focused on identifying the optimal city for Google's first flagship retail store in Europe/Asia and designing a full go-to-market launch strategy.

This was a group academic project completed as part of the Digital Marketing course at Kozminski University (Warsaw, 2025).

---

## Objective

Select the most strategically viable city for a Google flagship store based on quantitative market criteria, then design the store concept, target personas, and full SEM/display advertising launch strategy.

---

## What Was Analyzed

- Global market trends and e-commerce data across candidate cities
- Consumer personas and shopping behavior segmentation
- Product-market fit and brand synergy assessment
- Retail district viability and location potential
- Tourism and foot traffic volumes

---

## Methodology

**City Evaluation Framework** — A weighted scoring matrix across 5 criteria:

| Criteria | Hamburg | Milan | Kuala Lumpur |
|---|---|---|---|
| E-commerce Conversion Potential | 4 | 3 | 4 |
| Tourism & Foot Traffic | 4 | 5 | 5 |
| Tech & Device Adoption | 5 | 3 | 4 |
| Retail District Viability | 4 | 5 | 4 |
| Brand Engagement & Online Interest | 5 | 4 | 4 |
| **Total Score** | **22** | **20** | **21** |

**Winner: Hamburg** — highest overall score (22/25), strongest tech adoption and brand engagement metrics.

---

## Key Deliverables

### 1. Consumer Persona Framework
Three data-informed personas developed for the Hamburg market:
- **Julian the Creator** (29, freelance content creator, early tech adopter)
- **Lea the Future Professional** (21, Gen Z student, sustainability-conscious)
- **The Schmidt Family** (42, IT professional household, smart home loyalists)

### 2. Store Concept
*"The Future of Google, in Real Life"* — a disruptive, experiential showroom integrating AI/AR demos, Smart Home live zones, and interactive product displays.

### 3. SEM Campaign Plan
Three-phase paid search strategy:

| Campaign | Goal | Budget Split | KPIs |
|---|---|---|---|
| Brand Awareness | Pre-launch reach | 40% | Impressions, CTR, brand lift |
| Product Conversions | Drive Pixel/Nest sales | 35% | ROAS, conversion rate |
| Local Activation | Store foot traffic | 25% | Store visits, direction clicks |

### 4. Display Advertising Plan
Multi-platform campaign across Google Display Network, YouTube, and Meta — with three phases: Tease (1 month pre-launch), Launch Week Push, and Monthly Highlights.
